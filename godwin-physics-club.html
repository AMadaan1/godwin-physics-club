Why this exists

Physics C isn't part of Godwin's current course lineup, and there isn't currently an active space just for people who like physics — so we're building one. It's for anyone curious about how things work, whether that means grinding F=ma and USAPhO problems or just dropping in because you're bored on a Tuesday. Same club, no pressure either way.

What we'll actually do

Three simple things. Come to one, come to all.

🏆

Physics Bowls

Team trivia built on F=ma-style problems — the actual U.S. Physics Olympiad qualifier. Fun scoring, real problem-solving, no calculus needed.

🧪

Small Labs

Hands-on experiments with whatever we've got — measuring, guessing wrong, trying again.

🎤

Guest Talks

Real people doing real physics, dropping by to talk shop and take questions.

The serious track

If you want to actually compete, this is real.

Bowls and labs are the on-ramp. If competing is what you're actually here for, we're building real prep alongside everything else — not a footnote.

F=ma is algebra-based and open to anyone right now — no calculus required. It's also the qualifying exam for USAPhO, so once your math catches up, that's the natural next step.

Based on input from our charter members, we'll shape this to actually serve the people who show up for it — not a one-size-fits-all program handed down from the top.
